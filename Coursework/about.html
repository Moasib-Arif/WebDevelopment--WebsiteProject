Summary

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter and pop star who was first discovered by a record executive on accident. Since then, Justin Bieber has become one of the most commercially successful artists in the world with more than 150 billion records sold. In addition, he had won dozens of awards for his work. Justin is married to Hailey Baldwin, now Hailey Bieber.

Career

Justin Bieber was born on March 1, 1994 in Ontario, Canada to parents Jeremy and Pattie. Justin has three younger half-siblings on his father’s side and one step sister.

Justin Bieber grew up attending a French-language immersion school and learned how to play the piano, drums, trumpet and guitar.

He first started to get popular when he sang Ne-Yo’s “So Sick” for a local singing competition in 2007 at the yung age of 12 and placed second. His mother uploaded the video for family and friends, and kept uploading videos of Justin singing covers of songs, and slowly he grew in popularity.
